Bio

My name is Jar Hsuan Sherry Wang , a Taiwanese born content strategist who is passionate about utilizing different forms of media platforms to promote and raise awareness of different social topics. Focusing on media art, I mainly explore on graphic design, game design, UX/UI design, videography and virtual reality to represent ideas in an effective way. In different projects, I am mainly in charge of storyboarding, content analysis, ensuring meaningful project goals and pinpointing problems by giving thoughtful suggestions.

I strive to push myself to use emerging technology (like VR) to construct messages with the understanding of design principles and integration of other disciplines like UX/UI design, game design, graphic design and videography.
