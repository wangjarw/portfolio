Grid Structure

How the grid strucuture changes throughout different sizes is demostrated in main navigation, product listing and colour swatches.Normally in bigger sized screen, the underlying grid consists 6 colunms and for the most of the time, the content occupies 4 columns (about 60% of the width). On the other hand, the grid structure becomes 2 columns when the screen shrinks into middle size and then eventually eveything gets into a single column in the smallest size.

Colour Swatches

The colour combination I decide to go for is gray, blue, purple and amber colour. Due to the facts that the target audience for this company is mainly for people from the middle age to elderly people, the overall design should look calm and believable. This consideration is the reason why blue is selected as the main colour scheme. Although green is typically used to represent organic healthy food but colour blindness is being taking into consideration. Hence, it is avoided. Most of the sections would be in gray and only the parts that are emphasized or the bigger headers would be in blue to draw attention. High contrast is the basic idea I am aiming for. Hyperlinks would also be in colour and underlined.[some info can back up this ideas and these would be included in the citation page.]
