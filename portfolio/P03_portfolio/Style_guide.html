Colour Swatches

Since the position that I am aiming for is content strategist, I think it would be great to use yellow, green and red-- hightlight colours that are typically used for grouping content as my main colour scheme. On the other hand, since most of the planning happen on paper so this is also why for the texts and the headings, they will be in black.
